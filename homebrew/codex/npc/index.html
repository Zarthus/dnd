Fleabag

Human Unknown

Background Fleabag is a common beggar - found on the streets asking for coin and in general looking miserable. However: There is a special twist. Fleabag is actually of another plane and is merely tricking people. Fleabag believes that good deeds get rewarded, and bad deeds get punished severely. When people are good to him, good fortune will befall them. When people treat him poorly, terrible fortune will befall them.
Features & Traits They smell awful, torn clothing, and several scars - but an overall friendly attitude that only changes when others do not treat them nicely. They take on the appearance of an old masculine human, but most definitely are not. Fleabag can be killed very easily - but they will re-appear at some point in the future and appear fully alive again. Upon being murdered - a hefty curse will befall on the murderer(s), and the surrounding area will start to rot. Their memory stays intact throughout death and they remember and may even haunt specific people if they have been especially harsh to them.
Spells Illusion magic
Message
Equipment Tattered clothing
